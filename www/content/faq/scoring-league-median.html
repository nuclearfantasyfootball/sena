Extra Game Against League Median

An additional setting that can enabled in fantasy football leagues is extra game against league median, where each week teams have an additional matchup against the league median score. This means that in addition to a regular weekly matchup against another team, teams also have a matchup against the median score of all teams in the league. Enabling the option of an extra game each week against the league median applies to the regular season only.

Impact on Standings

Enabling an extra game each week against league median awards top performing teams with an additional win, while the teams scoring less than the median receive a loss. The purpose of implementing this setting is to reward high-scoring teams for the weekly performance, even if the team lost in a tough head-to-head matchup.

Extra game against league median helps create a more fair option for teams when they finish in the top half in weekly scoring, but still lost the head-to-head matchup.

Adding a league median minimizes the impact of luck in head-to-head matchups. Luck is still a factor in fantasy football, and enabling this setting does not eliminate luck, but it does create a more balanced approach to the league standings based on how they perform.

Calculate League Median

The league median is calculated by considering weekly scores of all teams in a league, and taking the average of the two teams in the middle of the scoring distribution. For example, in a 10-team league, the median is calculated by averaging the 5th and 6th highest scores of the week. If the 5th highest score for the week is 120 points and the 6th highest score is 115 points, the league median takes the average of these two scores.

\[ \text{League Median} = \frac{120 + 115}{2} = 117.5 \]

In this example, the league median score for the week is 117.5 points. Teams that score above 117.5 points would win an extra matchup for the week, and teams that score below would lose, creating the possibility of a team going 2-0 in a week, 1-1, or 0-2.

Possibility of a Tie

In the event that a team scores exactly the same as the league median, a tie is issued for the week. This is an extremely rare occurrence, but it is possible.
