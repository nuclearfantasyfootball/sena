League ID

A Sleeper league ID can be used to load league data and information. There are two ways you can obtain a Sleeper league ID. The first is by navigating to the Sleeper website, and the second is by navigating to general settings in a league in the Sleeper app.

Website

On web applications, navigate to the Sleeper website. Once you login, it will load up a league or navigate to one. The web address should now have a portion that shows an 18-digit numeric string, which is your Sleeper league ID.

https://sleeper.com/leagues/ 1190192546172342272

Mobile App

On the mobile app, go to a league after you log in. In the top right, there should be a settings button. Navigate to General and at the bottom of general settings should be an option that says COPY LEAGUE ID.
